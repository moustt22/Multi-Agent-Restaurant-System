RESERVATIONS
------------
- Reservations recommended, especially on weekends.
- Tables held 15 minutes past booking time.
- Cancel at least 2 hours in advance.
- Groups of 8+ need 24 hours notice.

REFUND POLICY
-------------
- Full refund if cancelled 24+ hours before booking.
- 50% refund if cancelled 2-24 hours before.
- No refund for no-shows or cancellations under 2 hours.
- Food complaints: full replacement or store credit.

LOYALTY PROGRAM - NOVA REWARDS
--------------------------------
- Earn 1 point per $1 spent.
- Silver (0-499 pts): 10% discount.
- Gold (500-999 pts): 15% discount + free dessert monthly.
- Platinum (1000+ pts): 20% discount + priority reservations + free birthday meal.
- Double points on Tuesdays. Triple points on your birthday month.
- Minimum 100 points to redeem. Points expire after 12 months inactive.

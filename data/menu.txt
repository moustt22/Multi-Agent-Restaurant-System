EVENT HOSTING
-------------

NovaBite offers private event hosting for:
- Birthday parties
- Corporate meetings
- Engagement dinners

Premium Catering Package includes:
- 3 main dishes
- 2 appetizers
- 2 desserts
- Beverage package
- Dedicated event coordinator

Minimum booking: 20 guests.


REFUND POLICY
-------------

Refunds allowed within 24 hours for catering bookings.
Event cancellations require 48-hour notice.
No refunds for completed dine-in orders.
